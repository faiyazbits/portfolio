## About Section

Most performance problems aren't caused by lack of resources.  
They're caused by not knowing where the real bottleneck is.

I'm a full-stack engineer and solutions architect with 10+ years of experience across government, healthcare, fintech, and public transit — and I've spent most of that time diagnosing systems that were slow, expensive, or both.

As Technical Lead at Provility, I've delivered performance fixes across every layer of the stack:

- Reduced a compliance form's load time from 14s to 4s (71% reduction) by rebuilding the SurveyJS expression engine with async microtask queuing and expression caching
- Cut patient data upload time from 300 minutes to 30 minutes (90% reduction) with a Node.js streaming pipeline and chunk-level exponential backoff
- Eliminated ~6s drag-and-drop lag in a Canadian government e-form builder by replacing a nested recursive tree with a flat linked-list structure — response dropped to under 1s
- Eliminated browser memory crashes on 10-year backtesting datasets with a debounced progressive rendering pipeline
- Achieved sub-millisecond route lookups for a Danish public transit platform by building a Java in-memory GTFS cache layer and reactive Kotlin coroutine APIs

Every one of these was a system-level diagnosis — not a surface patch.

Now I help SaaS teams do the same through a focused 30-day Performance Optimization Sprint: identify root causes, prioritize the highest-impact fixes, and guide measurable improvements — without rewriting the system.

AWS Certified Solutions Architect. 5+ years of remote delivery across the US, Canada, Denmark, and Australia.
